EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on the date of execution by and between TechCorp Industries ("Company") and the Employee, effective immediately upon signing.

1. EMPLOYMENT TERM AND TERMINATION

The Company may terminate this employment agreement at any time without providing any notice period or cause. The Employee shall have no right to severance pay or benefits upon termination. During the first 90 days of employment (probation period), either party may terminate employment without any notice requirement. After the probation period, the employer may terminate upon discovery of any breach or unsatisfactory performance without written notice.

2. COMPENSATION AND DEDUCTIONS

The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of $5,000. The Company reserves the right to deduct from the Employee's salary any amounts the Company deems appropriate, including but not limited to equipment costs, training expenses, or damages to company property, without requiring the Employee's written consent each time.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND WORK PRODUCT

All intellectual property, inventions, and work products created by the Employee, whether during work hours or outside work hours, shall be the sole property of the Company. This includes any personal projects, side work, or inventions developed using personal resources, provided they relate in any way to the Company's business. The Employee waives all rights to ownership.

4. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

The Employee agrees not to work for any competing business within a 500-mile radius for a period of 5 years after leaving employment. The Employee further agrees not to solicit or recruit any Company employees or approach any Company clients for a period of 10 years after employment ends.

5. UNILATERAL MODIFICATION

The Company reserves the sole discretion to modify this Agreement at any time without the Employee's consent. The Employee has no right to negotiate or approve any changes to the terms of employment.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

All information disclosed to the Employee, whether proprietary or not, shall be treated as confidential and may not be disclosed without Company permission. This applies to all information without any exceptions for already-public information, information the Employee knew prior to employment, or independently developed information.

7. INDEMNIFICATION

The Employee agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company from any and all losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including attorney fees) arising from any claim, whether or not caused by the Employee's actions, negligence, or willful misconduct.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through mandatory binding arbitration rather than court proceedings. The Employee waives the right to bring a class-action lawsuit. The arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding.

9. LIABILITY LIMITATION

The Company shall not be liable for any indirect, consequential, incidental, special, or punitive damages, including but not limited to loss of profits, even if the Company has been advised of the possibility of such damages.

10. AUTO-RENEWAL

This employment agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless the Employee provides written notice of non-renewal 60 days before the end of the current term. Failure to provide timely notice shall result in automatic renewal.

11. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Singapore and disputes shall be resolved in Singapore courts. The Employee agrees that any legal action must be brought in Singapore, regardless of where the Employee resides or works.

12. ASSIGNMENT

The Company reserves the right to assign this Agreement to any successor, parent company, or entity without the Employee's consent. The Employee has no right to object to any assignment.

13. AFTERWORD

The Employee waives all right to file a class action lawsuit and agrees to pursue only individual arbitration for any claims. Upon termination, the Employee shall return all work-related materials and agrees to delete all copies of any work products from personal devices.

14. FORCE MAJEURE

In the event of circumstances beyond control (such as pandemic, natural disaster, or war), the Company is relieved of all obligations under this Agreement. However, the Employee remains fully obligated to perform all duties.

15. PENALTY CLAUSE

If the Employee violates any term of this Agreement, the Employee agrees to pay the Company a liquidated damages penalty of $50,000, regardless of actual harm caused.

This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. The Employee acknowledges full understanding of these terms and waives any claim of misunderstanding or unconscionability.
